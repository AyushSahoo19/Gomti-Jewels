WHO WE ARE

Welcome to Gomti Jewels — dedicated to giving you the finest handmade jewellery with a focus on customer service and uniqueness. Founded in 1975 by Sh. Om Prakash Soni in Johari Bazar, Jaipur.

Our passion from day one was to introduce uniquely designed jewellery with the South Indian temple theme — a tradition upheld for 44+ years across all major cities of India.

Known for our unique designs and, above all, our integrity — our entire collection of Necklets, Earrings, and Bangles can be crafted in 5–8 business days.
